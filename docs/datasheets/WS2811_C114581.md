## Pin Descriptions (SOP-8)

| Pin # | Symbol | Function |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | OUTR | Red channel constant-current PWM output (open-drain, max 20 V) |
| 2 | OUTG | Green channel constant-current PWM output (open-drain, max 20 V) |
| 3 | OUTB | Blue channel constant-current PWM output (open-drain, max 20 V) |
| 4 | GND | Signal and power ground |
| 5 | DO | Serial data output — cascaded to next IC's DIN |
| 6 | DIN | Serial data input from MCU or previous IC's DO (max 9.5 V) |
| 7 | NC | No connect |
| 8 | VDD | Logic supply (3.5 V – 5.7 V); connect via series resistor Rd from LED supply rail |

| Symbol | Condition | Min | Max |

## Typical Application Circuit Component Values

| Supply Voltage | LEDs per Channel | Rd (VDD series resistor) | Ci (VDD decoupling) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **5 V** | 1 LED | **150 Ω** | **1 µF** |
| **12 V** | 3 LEDs (series) | **4.7 kΩ** | **1 µF** |
| **24 V** | 6 LEDs (series) | **10 kΩ** | **1 µF** |

> **Note:** At 24 V, additional current-setting resistors (240 Ω × 3, 560 Ω × 1) are placed in series with each OUT pin to set channel current correctly.

## Design Notes & Limitations

- **VDD must be derived from the LED supply via Rd.** The internal zener eliminates the need for an external regulator for supplies ≤ 24 V.
- **DIN is clamped to 9.5 V max.** Do not drive DIN directly from a 12 V or 24 V logic source; use a level-appropriate MCU (3.3 V / 5 V logic).
- **OUTR/G/B pins withstand up to 20 V** and include built-in short-circuit and over-voltage protection; a momentary short to 24 V will not destroy the output stage.
- **Data reshaping on every node:** Each cascaded IC regenerates and re-transmits the waveform, eliminating cumulative signal distortion. Point-to-point interconnect may be up to **4 m** without additional buffering.
- **Maximum cascade depth:** ≥ 1024 nodes at 30 frames/second refresh.
- **RESET must be asserted (DIN low ≥ 280 µs)** to latch and apply new PWM data to outputs; outputs hold their last value until RESET is received.
- **Channel outputs have staggered turn-on** to reduce simultaneous switching noise and power-supply ripple.
- **No external oscillator required** — internal 800 kHz RC oscillator is used.
- Place **Ci (1 µF) as close as possible to pin 8 (VDD)** to suppress supply transients during switching.
