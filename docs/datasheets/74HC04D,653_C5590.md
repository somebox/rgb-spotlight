```
        V_CC (2.0V – 6.0V)
          |
         [C1] 100nF  ← Bypass capacitor (ceramic, place close to pin 14)
         [C2] 10µF   ← Bulk capacitor (electrolytic, per board section)
          |
    Pin 14 (VCC) ─────┤
    Pin 7  (GND) ─────┴──── GND

    Signal Input
       │
      [R1] 1kΩ – 10kΩ  ← Current limiting resistor (if V_IN > V_CC)
       │
    Pin 1 (1A) ──► Pin 2 (1Y) → Inverted Output

                               [C_L] ≤ 50 pF load capacitance
```

## Design Notes & Limitations

> ⚠️ **Critical Design Considerations**

1. **Decoupling**: Place 100 nF ceramic capacitor as close as possible to pin 14 (V_CC) and pin 7 (GND). Add 10 µF bulk capacitor per power rail section.

2. **Input Overdrive Protection**: Inputs include internal clamp diodes. Use a **current-limiting resistor (1 kΩ typical)** when input voltages may exceed V_CC or go below GND to keep clamping current below ±20 mA absolute max.

3. **Unused Inputs**: **Never leave inputs floating.** Tie unused gate inputs to V_CC or GND through a resistor (10 kΩ typical) to prevent oscillation and excess I_CC.

4. **Input Transition Rate**: Slow input signals (>139 ns/V @ 4.5 V) may cause oscillation or increased power dissipation. Add a Schmitt-trigger buffer upstream if input edges are slow.

5. **Output Drive**: Maximum sustained output current is **±25 mA per pin** (absolute max). Do not exceed **±5.2 mA** under normal operating conditions at 6 V.

6. **Power Dissipation Derating**: SOIC-14 (SOT108-1) P_tot derates at **10.1 mW/°C above 100°C**. At 125°C: P_tot_max ≈ 500 − (10.1 × 25) = **247.5 mW**.

7. **Latch-Up Immunity**: Exceeds 100 mA per JESD 78 Class II Level B — robust against transient overcurrent events.

8. **74HC vs 74HCT**: This part is **74HC04** (CMOS-level inputs). The 74HCT04 variant accepts TTL-level inputs (V_IH = 2.0 V min) and requires 4.5 V–5.5 V supply only. Do **not** mix the two variants without verifying input thresholds.

9. **High-Frequency Use**: At 6 V with 50 pF load, propagation delay is 14 ns (max, 125°C). For faster switching, reduce C_L or increase V_CC within rated range.
