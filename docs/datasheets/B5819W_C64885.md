## Important Design Notes & Limitations

- **Power Derating:** P_D derates linearly from 500 mW at 25°C to **0 mW at 125°C**. At 85°C ambient, maximum allowable dissipation is approximately ~250 mW.
- **Leakage Temperature Sensitivity:** Reverse leakage (I_R) increases by **~10× from 25°C to 100°C** — critical for high-temperature applications and battery circuits.
- **Junction Capacitance:** C_j = 110 pF (typ) at zero bias; drops sharply with reverse voltage. Limits use in high-frequency switching above ~50 MHz.
- **Surge Handling:** 25 A peak surge is rated at **8.3 ms (single pulse)** only — not repetitive.
- **Not for life-support systems** without express written approval from the manufacturer.
- **Thermal Management:** The 160°C/W R_θJA rating is **only valid with a 5×5 cm copper pad**; smaller pads will significantly increase junction temperature.
- **Forward Voltage Caution:** Maximum V_F at 1 A is **0.90 V** (significantly higher than typical 0.60 V) — use max value for worst-case power loss budgeting.
