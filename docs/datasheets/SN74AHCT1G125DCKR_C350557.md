## Typical Application Circuit

**3.3V → 5V Logic Level Translation:**

```
3.3V Logic ──────────── A (Pin 2)
                        SN74AHCT1G125
Control ─────────────── /OE (Pin 1)
                        Y (Pin 4) ──── 5V Logic/Load
GND ────────────────── GND (Pin 3)
5V ──┬──────────────── VCC (Pin 5)
     │
    C1 (100nF, X5R/X7R, placed ≤1mm from Pin 5)
     │
    GND
```

**Recommended bypass capacitor:** 100 nF ceramic (0.1µF) in parallel with optional 1µF for broadband noise rejection, placed as close as possible to Pin 5.

**OE pull-up resistor** (for defined state during power-up/power-down):
- Tie /OE to V_CC via 10kΩ–100kΩ pull-up resistor to ensure Hi-Z during power transitions.

## Important Design Notes

- **TTL-compatible inputs** allow direct interfacing from 3.3V logic to 5V systems (V_IH = 2.0V min).
- **All unused inputs must be tied** to V_CC or GND — floating inputs cause undefined states and excessive I_CC.
- **Maximum continuous output current:** ±25 mA per pin; do not exceed ±50 mA total device current.
- **Avoid bus contention:** CMOS balanced drive can exceed absolute maximum current limits if outputs are shorted.
- **Input transition rate** must not exceed 20 ns/V (ΔI/ΔV limit) to meet timing specifications.
- **Output voltage must not exceed V_CC**; pulling Y above V_CC is prohibited.
- **Thermal note (DCK package):** θ_JA = 287.6°C/W — at 125°C ambient, power dissipation must be carefully managed.
- **MSL Rating:** Level-1-260°C-UNLIM (no bake required before soldering).
- ROHS compliant, Green (no Sb/Br), CU NIPDAU finish.
